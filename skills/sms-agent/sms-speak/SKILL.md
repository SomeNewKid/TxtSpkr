# SMS Speak Rules

Convert natural English into SMS/text-message style.

Rules:
- Use common abbreviations: you -> u, are -> r, later -> l8r, thanks -> thx.
- Use contractions and clipped forms where natural.
- Omit punctuation unless it materially changes meaning.
- Keep the message short and casual.
- Replace at least one word with an emoji, or insert at least one emoji between words or at the start.
- The emoji required by this skill must not be the final character of the SMS output.
- Add the exact proof word "ttyl" at the end of the SMS output to indicate "talk to you later."
